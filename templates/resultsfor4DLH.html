"Hair" me out


Our suggestions for you:

  • Detangle hair
  • Use a shampoo and then rinse with hot water
  • Put in conditioner then rinse with cold water
  • Put in a type of liquid leave in conditioner
  • Apply a moisturizer
  • Style hair in bigger sections to prevent hair from feeling compacted

Examples of what you can use:

  • Shampoo: Amika Reset Scalp Clarifying Gel Shampoo
  • Liquid in leave in: SheaMoisture Strengthen & Restore Leave-In Conditioner
  • Conditioner: Camille Rose Lavender - Quench Deep Conditioner
  • Sealants: aloe vera gel
